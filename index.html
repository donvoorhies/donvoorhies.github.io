About

I'm Don W. Voorhies — an educator, web and app developer, designer, and information communicator with 30+ years of experience creating digital solutions that connect technology, people, and purpose.

My work blends hands-on development, creative design, and strategic communication — from building web experiences, integrating AI tools and solutions, to leading development teams and teaching the next generation of digital professionals.

Driven by curiosity and a love for clarity, I enjoy translating complex technology into meaningful user experiences — whether through AI-driven tools and integration, web platforms, or educational initiatives that empower others to understand and use digital innovation.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about record collecting, (occasional) DJ'ing, and now photography (again!) — creative outlets that keep my perspective grounded in both art and rhythm.
